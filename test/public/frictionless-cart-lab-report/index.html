A pulley was attached to a flat table surface using table clamp. approximately 1 meter of string was cut from a spool and one end was tied to the top of the frictionless cart. The frictionless cart was placed upside down at the farthest point away from the pulley that would be necessary, and the other end of the string was run through the pulley and a looped knot was tied that was to be used to hang standard masses. Any excess string was cut in to ensure that the force of gravity would not decrease due to the string reaching the ground in the middle of the trial.
